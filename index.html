About Vynxcode

Who I Am Behind The Code

Hey! I’m Tiffany, professionally known online as Vynxcode. I’m a front-end web developer currently working towards my Full-Stack certification. I specialize in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, combining creativity and technical skills to build modern, user-friendly, and visually engaging websites.

My hands-on experience so far includes building and maintaining a band's website, as well as developing personal and class projects to practice and showcase my skills. While most of my work has been small-scale, I'm eager to collaborate with both small businesses and larger companies to deliver fast, accessible, and visually engaging websites.

Fueled by sweet tea and a love for problem-solving, turning tricky problems into organized, functional code. I’m committed to delivering results that help clients grow their online presence, and I'm always exploring new tech to improve my skills, my craft, and the online visuals that capture an audience.

I have experience with e-commerce and CMS platforms. My background includes working with Shopify, Wix, Ionos, Hostinger, and eBay. Where I've managed product listings, optimized SEO, embedded custom code, and maintained websites with updates, new features, and more. Beyond development, I design logos, write SEO-driven content, and leverage AI tools to streamline workflows and enhance my coding solutions.
